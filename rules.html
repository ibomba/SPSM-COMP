Rules

Refrees: Ramy & Makarios

1. If a player is not present for their scheduled ping-pong game, they will be automatically considered for conceding the match.

2. Players must serve behind the table.

4. Matches are best of 3 games.

3. Games are out of 11 points.

4. Tie breakers are played: 2 point difference.

5. Tie breaker score is always switched to 11-10.

6. If the match score is 2-1, players get their scores from their top 2 games.

7. Wild Cards are players who failed to qualify through normal play but had a higher performance than every other unqualified player. 2 Wild Cards qualify for the second bracket.
